About Manchester United

Manchester United Football Club, commonly referred to as Man United, is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England. They compete in the Premier League, the top tier of English football.

  • Founded: 1878, Newton Heath, Manchester, United Kingdom
  • Manager: Ruben Amorim
  • Arena/Stadium: Old Trafford
  • Locations: Trafford, United Kingdom; Old Trafford, Stretford, United Kingdom
  • Leagues: Premier League, UEFA Europa League, FA Cup, EFL Cup
  • Nicknames: The Red Devils, United
  • Training Ground: Carrington Training Centre
