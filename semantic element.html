About the Club

Manchester United is one of the most successful football clubs in England, based in Old Trafford, Manchester.

Club History

Founded in 1878 as Newton Heath LYR Football Club, Manchester United has won multiple Premier League titles and European trophies.

Old Trafford

Known as the "Theatre of Dreams", Old Trafford has been United's home stadium since 1910.
